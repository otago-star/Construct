Manulea Construction is a family-owned business established by Laiva’a Manulea after 20 years’ experience in the construction industry. Our goal is to build our reputation for reliability and exceptional workmanship while offering competitive pricing.

Services

We specialize in new builds, renovations (bathrooms, kitchens etc), fencing, concrete work, driveways and have extensive experience on large sites.
